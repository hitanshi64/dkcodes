browser. it includes aspects such as web design , web publishing, web programming,
and database managment. it is the creation of an application that works over the internet
i.e Website.

The word Web Development is made up of two words,that is:

  • WEB: It refers to website,web pages and anything that works over the internet.
  • devlopment: It refers to building the application from scratch
